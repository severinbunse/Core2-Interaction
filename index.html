Course Description

This course exposes students to interactive concepts and techniques for applications. It is an investigation in the interface, the mechanism, the controls and the aims of interactive works. Students will learn how to design and develop interactive projects and understand how to undertake research and direct their thinking process from brainstorming to the final outcome. They will be given the tools to conceive, plan and develop an interactive system and they will become aware of the importance of design’s role in contemporary media.

Project 1: Structure & Style (due February 20)

In this first unit we will focus on the fundamental relationship between structure and style as an organizing principle in design and on the web.
Create a responsive, multi-page website that publishes an existing text of your choice, visually amplifying its conceptual ideas through typography.
